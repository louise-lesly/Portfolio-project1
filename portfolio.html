Louise Lesley - My Portfolio Website

Through this website, Louise continues to build confidence in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, experimenting with clean layouts, animations, and interactivity. It’s more than a website—it's her digital fingerprint. A place where creativity meets skill, and where dreams begin to take shape.
