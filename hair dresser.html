Louise Lesley - Creative Hair Stylist

Louise Lesley is not just a hairdresser—she’s an artist with scissors. Her journey into hairstyling began with a love for transformation and expression. Louise takes pride in helping people feel confident by reinventing their look with personalized styles that suit their personality and lifestyle. Whether it’s braiding, cutting, or creative dye jobs, she ensures that each client walks away with a smile.

Her dedication to keeping up with modern trends, understanding hair textures, and using top-quality products has earned her recognition in her community. Louise believes hair care is about more than beauty—it's about confidence and identity. She brings patience, care, and style to every appointment.

Louise has worked with all types of hair and specializes in Afro-textured hair. From intricate protective styles to natural treatments, she merges tradition with trend. Her mobile salon service allows clients to enjoy professional care from the comfort of their homes. To her, every head is a new canvas.
